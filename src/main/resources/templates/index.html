About Pakakumi

Pakakumi is a Kenyan online betting game that offers sports and crash betting. It's a thrilling social online betting game where you can securely play for fun or to win a fortune. It's real-time, simple, and exciting where you bet on chart patterns. The rising and falling of the line chart is influenced by the number of players currently playing and their bet multiple using an algorithm called Provably fair.
